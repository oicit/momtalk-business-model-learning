Once Upon a Time... 📖
👦🛒
8-year-old Truett
sold sodas from a wagon!
🏠 In 1946, he opened a tiny restaurant called "Dwarf Grill" - it only had 10 seats!
🥪 In 1967, he invented the FIRST chicken sandwich and started Chick-fil-A!
⭐ The "A" in the name means "A-grade" - the BEST quality!
