003 The 9-Week Adventure 📅

Tap any week to peek inside! 👇

1
🎬 What IS the AI Economy?
April 2
+

"Generative AI" means AI that creates new stuff — pictures, stories, songs, even computer code. It's not just looking things up — it's actually making things that never existed before! 🎨

Your teacher walks you through the WHOLE AI world from bottom to top. Think of AI like a giant layer cake 🎂:

🎂 The AI Layer Cake
  • 🔌 Bottom layer: Chips (the physical brains)
  • ☁️ Middle layer: Cloud computers (where AI lives)
  • 🧠 Upper layer: AI models (like ChatGPT or Claude)
  • 📱 Top layer: Apps you actually use every day

The big question: WHO makes money at each layer? Who's winning? Who might lose? That's the mystery you'll be solving all 9 weeks! 🕵️

💡 Fun fact: AI companies made more money in 2024 than the entire global movie industry!
🗺️ Case Study: The AI map — preview of every company you'll study
2
🧠 The Chip Wizards
April 9
+

Every AI brain needs a special kind of computer chip called a GPU. Here's the wild part: GPUs were originally built for VIDEO GAMES 🎮 — to draw cool graphics fast. Then someone realized: the same math that draws explosions in Fortnite is EXACTLY what AI needs! Surprise hit! 💥

🧠 Cool things you'll learn:
  • Why one AI chip can cost $40,000+ 💸
  • Why ONE company (NVIDIA) makes almost ALL of them
  • How Groq built a totally different chip that's even faster
  • Why countries are racing to build chip factories
🤯 Fun fact: The chip in your phone has BILLIONS of tiny switches, each smaller than a virus!
🍳 Imagine this: A regular computer chip is like ONE chef cooking one meal at a time. A GPU is like 10,000 chefs cooking 10,000 meals at the SAME TIME. That's why AI loves them!
🏢 Case Studies: NVIDIA (the $3 trillion king) + Groq (the fast challenger)
3
🎙️ Robot Voices
April 16
+

ElevenLabs makes AI that can talk in ANY voice. Want a voice that sounds like a movie narrator? A friendly grandma? A scary villain? They've got it. They can even clone any real person's voice from just a few seconds of audio! 🎙️✨

🎤 Cool things you'll learn:
  • How AI learns to talk by listening to thousands of hours of people
  • How audiobooks, video games, and movies use AI voices now
  • Why it's AMAZING (helps blind people read books!) and a little SCARY (fake voices can trick people)
  • How they make money — companies pay per second of audio created
🌍 Imagine: One actor records their voice ONCE. Now AI can make them say ANYTHING — in 30 different languages — without them ever stepping into a studio again!
🎬 Fun fact: Some Hollywood movies now use AI voices for tiny background characters because it's WAY cheaper than hiring actors!
🏢 Case Study: ElevenLabs — the world leader in AI voice cloning
4
🏢 AI for Giant Companies
April 23
+

"Enterprise" is a fancy word for HUGE companies — like banks, hospitals, and stores with thousands of locations. They've been collecting data (information!) for DECADES. Databricks helps them dig through it all with AI. 📊

🏢 Cool things you'll learn:
  • What "data" really means — every order, every email, every photo ever!
  • A NEW idea called "Service as a Software" — instead of buying tools, you pay for RESULTS
  • Why big companies will spend BILLIONS on AI this year alone
  • Why selling to giant companies is super different from selling to regular people
🛒 Imagine: Walmart has data on EVERY single purchase ever made in their stores — billions of them! AI can spot patterns no human could ever notice. (Like: "people who buy diapers also buy beer at 9pm" — that's a real thing they found! 🤣)
💰 Fun fact: Databricks is worth over $60 BILLION dollars — more than many famous airlines!
🏢 Case Study: Databricks — the $60+ billion enterprise AI giant
5
🎁 The Mystery Visitor!
April 30
+

The biggest AI brains in the world come from "frontier labs" — companies like OpenAI (who built ChatGPT) and Anthropic (who built Claude — yes, the one writing this for you! 👋). These labs spend HUNDREDS OF MILLIONS of dollars to train just ONE AI! 💰

🚀 Cool things you'll learn:
  • Why training one AI costs $100 million+ (millions of GPU hours!)
  • How they make money back (you paying $20/month + companies paying to use them)
  • Why these labs are worth more than entire countries' economies
  • The big race — who will build "super-smart AI" first?
⚡ Wild fact: Training a single big AI model uses as much electricity as a small city for MONTHS!
🏆 It's like the Olympics — but instead of running races, companies are racing to build the smartest AI. The winner could change the whole world!
🏢 Case Studies: OpenAI (ChatGPT) + Anthropic (Claude) — the brain builders
6
📂 AI That Reads ALL Your Stuff
May 7
+

Imagine if YOUR school had an AI that read every textbook, every teacher note, every old test — and could answer ANY question about your school. That's what Applied Compute builds for big companies! 📚🔍

🧩 Cool things you'll learn:
  • Why a company's secret internal info is its biggest superpower
  • How to teach AI brand new knowledge it didn't learn from the internet
  • Why this is REALLY hard (info is messy, scattered, and often top-secret 🤫)
  • How smart companies turn their boring data into an AI superpower
🏥 Imagine: A new doctor joins a hospital. Normally it takes YEARS to learn how everything works. With a custom AI? She knows the whole hospital in DAYS. That's a HUGE deal!
🤖 Cool fact: Most regular AIs only know stuff from the public internet. But companies have WAY MORE info inside their own walls — and that's where the real money is!
🏢 Case Study: Applied Compute — custom AI brains for big businesses
7
🤖 Helper Robots (Agents!)
May 14
+

Here's the big difference: Chatbots TALK to you. Agents DO STUFF for you. Like: "Hey AI, book me a haircut next Tuesday at 4pm AND reorder my dog food." And it... just does it! 🐕✂️

🤖 Cool things you'll learn:
  • The huge difference between answering and acting
  • Should agents charge per task? Per month? Per RESULT? (Big question!)
  • Why agents are tricky — if they mess up, it can cost real money 😬
  • How the "behind the scenes" tech works (someone has to run those computers!)
📝 Imagine: An agent that does your whole homework process — checks the assignment, gathers info, drafts answers, even prints it out. (Don't worry — you still gotta actually LEARN the stuff yourself!)
💸 Fun fact: Some companies are testing agents that do the work of FULL-TIME employees — for less than $100/month. That's like hiring a tiny robot worker!
🏢 Case Study: Baseten — the engine room running AI agents at scale
8
⌨️ AI That Writes Code
May 21
+

"Code" is the secret language that makes apps and websites work. AI can now write code BY ITSELF. Just describe what you want — and boom, AI writes it! 💻✨ Vercel is the place where millions of websites live, so they have a front-row seat to this revolution.

⌨️ Cool things you'll learn:
  • How AI learned to code (it studied millions of programs!)
  • What happens to coder jobs — fewer jobs? Or MORE? (Could be both!) 🤔
  • Why building websites just got 100x faster
  • The new job: humans who DIRECT the AI to build stuff (you'd be GREAT at this!)
🚀 Wild fact: Some teenagers have built apps used by MILLIONS of people — without writing a single line of code themselves! They just told AI what to build.
🎨 It's like the difference between painting a picture and DESCRIBING a picture for someone else to paint. The "describer" is becoming the most important job!
🏢 Case Study: Vercel — home to millions of AI-built websites
9
🎤 Show Your Big Idea!
May 28
+

The final week is SHARK TANK STYLE 🦈! Students get on stage and pitch their own ideas about AI companies. There are two kinds of bets:

📊 The Two Bets:
  • 📈 LONG = "I bet this company will WIN BIG and their stock will go UP!"
  • 📉 SHORT = "I bet this company will FAIL and their stock will go DOWN!"
🎤 Cool things you'll learn:
  • How to RESEARCH a company like a real Wall Street pro
  • How to spot which AI companies are real winners vs. just hype 🎪
  • How to actually PRESENT your ideas (public speaking is scary at first!)
  • Why being WRONG sometimes is totally fine — even pros are wrong half the time!
💡 Inspiring fact: Some of the world's smartest investors started by "paper trading" as KIDS — just writing down their guesses on paper to see how they would have done!
🌟 Students present their LONG and SHORT pitches!
